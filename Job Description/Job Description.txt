You will work closely with different departments to understand data needs and deliver actionable insights.

Develop and maintain dashboards and reports using visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).

Utilize statistical methods to analyze data and generate predictive insights.

Communicate findings effectively to technical and non-technical audiences.

Support data-driven decision-making processes by providing reliable and timely data insights.

Assist in data governance and ensure compliance with data privacy regulations.
